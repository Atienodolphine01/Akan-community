Akan Community

The Akan community consists of unique group of people who have dominated the Ivory Coast and the Ghana regions of West Africa.
Some of the prominent personalities who are members of the group include Kwameh Nkuruma, Koffi Annan, Maria Balotelli and many more.

The Akan people of Ghana name their children after the day of the week they were born. These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person. Middle names have considerably more variety and can refer to their birth order, twin status, or an ancestor's middle name.
